üå™ Types of Disasters & Escaping Techniques

1. Earthquake

Escaping Techniques:

  • Drop, Cover and Hold under strong furniture.
  • Stay away from windows, glass and heavy objects.
  • Do not use lifts during or after an earthquake.
  • Move to an open ground after shaking stops.

2. Flood

Escaping Techniques:

  • Avoid walking or driving through floodwater.
  • Move valuables to higher floors.
  • Turn off electrical appliances immediately.
  • Evacuate early when warnings are issued.

3. Fire Accident

Escaping Techniques:

  • Crawl low under smoke to avoid inhalation.
  • Use fire exit routes, never elevators.
  • Stop, Drop, and Roll if clothes catch fire.
  • Use fire extinguishers if trained.
